How to Make Bubble Tea

Main Components

The Bubble Tea Supply website goes into more detail about the main components of bubble tea. Here, we just list the components and some examples of those components, which are also mentioned by Bubble Tea Supply.

  • Flavor: Flavored powder, Flavored punch/drink, flavored syrup, fruit purees or fresh fruit
  • Creamer: Powder creamer, half-n-half, full milk or other creamer
  • Sweetner: Sugar, sugar-free, simple sugar syrup, fructose, or honey
  • Liquid: Water, tea or milk
